BOOTROOM — Built-In Activities & Session Templates Reference
=============================================================


ACTIVITIES
==========

--- WARMUP ---

1. Dynamic Stretching
   Duration: 10 min | Intensity: 2/10
   Progressive dynamic stretches — hip circles, leg swings, high knees, butt kicks, lateral shuffles.

2. Rondo Warmup (4v1)
   Duration: 10 min | Intensity: 3/10
   Quick-touch possession circle. 4 players keep the ball from 1 defender in a small grid. Rotate on turnover.

3. Passing Gates
   Duration: 10 min | Intensity: 3/10
   Pairs pass through cone gates scattered across the area. Score a point for each completed pass through a gate. Move to a new gate after each pass.


--- TECHNICAL ---

4. 5v2 Rondo
   Duration: 15 min | Intensity: 5/10
   Possession drill in tight space. 5 attackers maintain possession against 2 defenders. Focus on body shape, first touch, and quick decision-making.

5. 1v1 Finishing
   Duration: 15 min | Intensity: 6/10
   Attackers receive a pass and go 1v1 against a defender with a shot on goal. Emphasize quick turn, change of pace, and clinical finishing.

6. Passing Combinations
   Duration: 15 min | Intensity: 4/10
   Rehearsed passing patterns — wall passes, overlaps, third-man runs, through balls. Progress from walking pace to game speed.

7. Crossing & Finishing
   Duration: 15 min | Intensity: 6/10
   Wide players deliver crosses into the box. Attackers time their runs for near post, far post, and cutback finishes.


--- TACTICAL ---

8. Pressing Triggers
   Duration: 20 min | Intensity: 7/10
   Team pressing on identified cues — backward pass, poor touch, sideline trap. Work in units to press as a coordinated block.

9. Build Out from Back
   Duration: 20 min | Intensity: 5/10
   GK distribution through defensive and midfield thirds. Focus on creating passing angles, movement off the ball, and progressing under pressure.

10. Transition Game
    Duration: 20 min | Intensity: 7/10
    Attack-to-defense and defense-to-attack transitions. On turnover, attacking team must press immediately while defending team looks to counter.

11. Defensive Shape
    Duration: 15 min | Intensity: 5/10
    Compact defensive lines. Practice sliding as a unit, covering, and maintaining distances between lines against ball movement.


--- GAME FORM ---

12. Small-Sided Game (4v4)
    Duration: 15 min | Intensity: 7/10
    High-tempo game in a reduced area with small goals. Emphasize quick transitions, tight control, and constant movement.

13. Full Scrimmage
    Duration: 25 min | Intensity: 8/10
    Match conditions on a full-size pitch. Apply the session focus in a realistic game environment. Coach can freeze play for coaching moments.

14. Conditioned Game
    Duration: 20 min | Intensity: 7/10
    Modified rules to reinforce the session theme — e.g., two-touch only, must play wide before shooting, goals from crosses count double.


--- FITNESS ---

15. Agility Circuit
    Duration: 15 min | Intensity: 8/10
    Ladder drills, cone weaves, hurdle hops, and short sprints. Rotate through stations with active recovery between sets.


--- PHYSICAL ---

16. Interval Sprints
    Duration: 15 min | Intensity: 9/10
    Repeated sprint efforts — 30m sprints with walk-back recovery. Progress to longer distances or shorter rest periods.


--- SET PIECES ---

17. Corner Kick Routines
    Duration: 15 min | Intensity: 4/10
    Rehearse attacking and defending corner variations. Near post flick-ons, far post runs, short corners, and zonal/man-marking assignments.


--- RECOVERY ---

18. Cool Down & Stretch
    Duration: 10 min | Intensity: 1/10
    Light jog followed by static stretching. Hold each stretch 20-30 seconds. Focus on hamstrings, quads, hip flexors, and calves.



SESSION TEMPLATES
=================

1. Heavy Technical — Rondos
   Type: Technical | Duration: 90 min | Intensity: 5/10
   Focus: Possession & Quick Passing
   Rondo-heavy session building from warmup possession circles through tight-space drills to a game-form finish.
   Activities:
     Warmup    — Rondo Warmup (4v1)
     Activity 1 — 5v2 Rondo
     Activity 2 — Passing Combinations
     Activity 3 — Small-Sided Game (4v4)
     Activity 4 — Cool Down & Stretch

2. Tactical — Pressing & Transitions
   Type: Tactical | Duration: 90 min | Intensity: 7/10
   Focus: High Press & Transition Play
   Shape-focused session working defensive pressing triggers and quick attack-to-defense transitions.
   Activities:
     Warmup    — Dynamic Stretching
     Activity 1 — Pressing Triggers
     Activity 2 — Transition Game
     Activity 3 — Conditioned Game
     Activity 4 — Cool Down & Stretch

3. Fitness Session
   Type: Fitness | Duration: 75 min | Intensity: 9/10
   Focus: Speed & Conditioning
   High-intensity fitness work combining agility circuits with repeated sprint efforts and a competitive finish.
   Activities:
     Warmup    — Dynamic Stretching
     Activity 1 — Agility Circuit
     Activity 2 — Interval Sprints
     Activity 3 — Small-Sided Game (4v4)
     Activity 4 — Cool Down & Stretch

4. Set Piece Focus
   Type: Tactical | Duration: 75 min | Intensity: 5/10
   Focus: Set Pieces & Finishing
   Rehearse corner, free kick, and throw-in routines with crossing and finishing practice.
   Activities:
     Warmup    — Passing Gates
     Activity 1 — Corner Kick Routines
     Activity 2 — Crossing & Finishing
     Activity 3 — 1v1 Finishing
     Activity 4 — Cool Down & Stretch

5. Game-Like Session
   Type: Tactical | Duration: 90 min | Intensity: 7/10
   Focus: Match Preparation
   Game-realistic session progressing from build-up play through conditioned games to a full scrimmage.
   Activities:
     Warmup    — Rondo Warmup (4v1)
     Activity 1 — Build Out from Back
     Activity 2 — Conditioned Game
     Activity 3 — Full Scrimmage
     Activity 4 — Cool Down & Stretch

6. Recovery Session
   Type: Recovery | Duration: 45 min | Intensity: 2/10
   Focus: Active Recovery
   Light session focused on recovery after a match or intense training block. Low intensity throughout.
   Activities:
     Warmup    — Dynamic Stretching
     Activity 1 — Passing Gates
     Activity 2 — Cool Down & Stretch
